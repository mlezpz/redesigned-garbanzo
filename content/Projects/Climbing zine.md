Idea:
- climbing is becoming ever more corporatized and mainstream. some disconnect and even butting heads of old school traditional dirtbag culture and current indoor boulder bro culture

- there exists a climbing zine already -- I haven't read it, but to judge the book by its cover, i think it leaves some gaps that this zine can fill.

	- indoor gymbro scene
	- new era/wave basically

- Nowadays most climbers aren't making it to the big walls outdoors, they don't have time for week or month-long excursions into the wild and don't have capacity to project outdoors
- This zine is for the people who love the sport for the community, the vibes, gamification aspect, the self improvement/gym aspect.
